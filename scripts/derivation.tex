\section{Fluid-Time Equivalence}
The coupling between the scalar field $\psi$ and the metric $g_{\mu\nu}$ is modified by the viscosity tensor $\eta$ of the Syrup:
\begin{equation}
    G_{\mu\nu} + \Lambda g_{\mu\nu} = 8\pi G (T_{\mu\nu} + \eta_{\mu\nu})
\end{equation}
Where $\eta$ is derived from the interaction of baryonic matter with the Dark Photon background.
